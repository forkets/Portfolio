Work History

Resume

Alex started his working life at the races. His first job was working at the bar at the Launceston Cup. After that, he moved to Melbourne in search of more permanent work. He spent some time with a recruitment agency working various catering events around Melbourne.

Alex went through a variety of hospitality jobs before finally obtaining a certificate in animal studies. From there he was able to gain employment with another recruitment agency, this time with a focus on animals.

He spent the next few years working casually at Monash University as an animal technician, caring for mice that would be later used in various medical research.This work ultimately let him to gaining a diploma of animal technology and moved on from Monash to WEHI (Walter and Eliza Hall Institute) in 2018 where he still works.
